You must apply caution when using papers from the old system.
If the course has remained similar today, the papers can be reused.
It is not recommended to use papers in a course that has undergone large changes. At best, the individual questions may be used selectively
Additionally, some subjects may have split into multiple subjects so you must check the other related subjects for past papers.
Note that stage 3 papers are the most useful; stage 2 reflects a lower difficulty.
